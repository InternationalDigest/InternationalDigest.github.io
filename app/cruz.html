Trump Trumped by Cruz in Iowa

Ted Cruz, one of the many non-Establishment candidates in the 2016 Presidental Election, has beaten Donald Trump and Ben Carson, two other outsiders, to become the first place Republican candidate in the crucial swing-state of Iowa (according to 3 polls). This peice on Cruz's newfound popularity in Iowa is written by John Smith, a reporter for the Associated Press.
