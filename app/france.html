Populist Faliure in France

The radical, anti-imigration, populist National Front has faultered at the final stage in the French regional elections, winning no regions in contrast to their success in the first round of the election. This in-depth analysis of this failiure and populism in general is written by Mitchell Palmer, the Executive Editor of the Internation Digest, and is exclusive to the Digest.
